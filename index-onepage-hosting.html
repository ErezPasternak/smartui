Secure Browsing

Secure Browsing isolates the organization’s web environment from malware that normally penetrates via browsing activity by using a virtual browser as a shield

Secure Browsing offers the freedom of web browsing while eliminating the threat of malware, such as Ransomware, back doors, keystroke loggers, and others. The solution integrates seamlessly into the standard IT environment and helps protect your existing IT investment, such as Firewall and Antivirus, by adding yet another important security layer. Employees can use their favorite web browser and have a native user experience: navigate to URLs, click links, watch videos and download files.

How does it work?

Instead of directly browsing the internet from their workstations, users are given access to the internet using browsers safely isolated in the DMZ. This means that no malware can impacting directly the user workstation. If any content is downloaded, whether knowingly or unknowingly, it is not allowed onto the internal network (LAN) where the real damage can occur and the threat is being contained at the DMZ.

E-Commerce Protection

E-Commerce Protection provides an effective shield from ongoing cyber-attacks to the corporate site.

Every commercial site, and especially those sites that handles online purchasing and banking, are being constantly bombarded by cyber-attacks that are attempting to steal financial data, such as credit card numbers, bank accounts info and such. There are common safety measures used by most, such as Firewalls, secure connections (https://) and encryption (Secured Socket Layer).

Additionally, organizations can also use a hosted browser protection layer that acts as a barrier between the attacks and the corporate site and helps contain the threats before they can penetrate and harm the site. From the end-user perspective this is all transparent.

How does it work?

The end-user opens up a browsing session to the target website of choice, which triggers a hosted browser session that is opened up in the background and is now displayed to the user seamlessly. This hosted browser session is initiated from within a safe zone (e.g., DMZ or cloud) and acts as a shield that protects the corporate site. In the event that a malicious cyber-attack is successful, it does not affect the actual corporate site, only the server farm that manages the virtual browser sessions.

Secure Access to Office PCs

Ensure Business continuity and disaster recovery with maximum security and from any device.

Many organizations forbid their employees from taking their office PCs outside the premises, due to the sensitive nature of the information they deal with, such as financial and health records. Keeping the devices within the secure environments is one of many safety measures that are common practice. However, there are various situations that require these corporate employees to securely access their office PCs from afar, in order to handle an urgent matter or during circumstances that prevent them from physically reaching the office. In these situation they may be using multiple devices and need maximum security as well as great flexibility.

How does it work?

Using a secure, browser-based access solution, corporate employees use their credentials to login, from any device, and access their physical office PC. This secure connection is enabled using a secure gateway that enables remote access while ensuring that no data from within the browsing device of choice is able to harm the corporate network and no data from the office PC is cached to the device, thus ensuring data protection that compliance with the highest regulations, while ensuing business continuity as required.
